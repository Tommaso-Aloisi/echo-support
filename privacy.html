Introduction

Welcome to Echo ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Echo (the "App").

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Username, email address, password (encrypted), profile photo, bio, date of birth, and nationality
  • Profile Content: Answers to profile questions, posts, and personal information you choose to share
  • Messages: Content of messages you send and receive through the App
  • AI Agent Data: AI agents you create, including names, descriptions, system prompts, and configurations
  • Calendar Data: Events you create and manage in the integrated calendar
  • Voice Calls: Metadata about voice calls (duration, participants, timestamps) but not call content
  • Media: Photos, videos, documents, and audio files you upload or share

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features you use, interactions with AI agents, time spent in the App
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, error logs, and diagnostic information
  • Performance Data: Crash reports and performance metrics to improve the App

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App's functionality
  • Enable messaging, voice calls, and group conversations
  • Process and deliver AI agent responses using third-party AI services (Mistral AI)
  • Personalize your experience and provide relevant content
  • Send you notifications about messages, friend requests, and app updates
  • Maintain the security and integrity of the App
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features and performance
  • Respond to your support requests and communications
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and prevent fraud or abuse

How We Share Your Information

With Other Users

  • Profile Information: Your username, profile photo, and bio are visible to your connections
  • Messages: Messages are delivered to intended recipients (individuals or group members)
  • Posts: Posts you publish are visible to your connections
  • AI Agents: If you share an agent to the marketplace, its name, description, and functionality are visible to all users

With Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf:

  • Mistral AI: AI agent messages are sent to Mistral AI for processing and generating responses. Mistral AI processes this data according to their privacy policy
  • Firebase: For push notifications and authentication services
  • LiveKit: For voice call infrastructure and real-time communication
  • Cloud Hosting: For data storage and app infrastructure (Railway)

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests, such as court orders or government investigations.

Business Transfers

If Echo is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Messages: Stored indefinitely unless you delete them
  • AI Agent Responses: Response metadata is stored; actual AI-generated content may be processed but not permanently stored by third parties
  • Deleted Data: Deleted data is removed from active systems within 30 days but may persist in backups for up to 90 days

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

  • Profile Settings: Update your profile information, photo, and bio at any time
  • Privacy Controls: Control who can see your profile and send you messages
  • Block Users: Block users to prevent them from contacting you
  • Archive Conversations: Archive conversations to hide them from your main list
  • Delete Content: Delete messages, posts, and AI agents you've created

Data Portability

You can request a copy of your data by contacting us at privacy@echo.social.

Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time through the App settings. This will permanently delete your:

  • Profile information and posts
  • Messages and conversation history
  • AI agents you created
  • Calendar events
  • All associated data

Note: Account deletion is irreversible.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • JWT (JSON Web Token) authentication for secure access
  • Encryption in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Encrypted password storage using industry-standard hashing
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Access controls limiting employee access to user data

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Echo is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at privacy@echo.social.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Third-Party AI Services

Mistral AI Processing: When you use AI agents, your messages are sent to Mistral AI for processing. Mistral AI:

  • Processes messages to generate AI responses
  • Does not use your data to train their models (as per their enterprise agreement)
  • Applies their own privacy and security measures
  • Retains data according to their privacy policy

Learn more about Mistral AI's data practices: Mistral AI Privacy Policy

Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notify you through the App or via email for significant changes
  • Post the updated policy on our support website

Your continued use of Echo after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@echo.social
  • Support: support@echo.social
  • Support Page: support.echo.social

Your California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to say no to the sale of personal information
  • Right to access your personal information
  • Right to equal service and price, even if you exercise your privacy rights
  • Right to request deletion of your personal information

Note: We do not sell your personal information.

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of access to your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@echo.social.

Consent

By using Echo, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
