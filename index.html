Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an AI agent?

Tap the Agents tab, then tap "Create Agent". Enter a name, description, and system prompt to define your agent's behavior.

What's the difference between AUTO and ON-CALL agents?

AUTO agents respond to every message in conversations where they're activated. ON-CALL agents only respond when you specifically summon them.

How do I delete a conversation?

Swipe left on the conversation in your list and tap Archive.

How do I report a problem?

Email us at support@echo.social with details about the issue.
