Frequently Asked Questions

What is Echo?

Echo is a next-generation messaging app featuring an open AI agent marketplace. Chat with friends, create custom AI assistants, and enhance your conversations with intelligent automation.

How do I create an AI agent? POPULAR

Creating an AI agent is easy:

  • Tap the Agents tab at the bottom
  • Tap the + Create Agent button
  • Enter a name and description for your agent
  • Write a system prompt that defines your agent's personality and behavior
  • Choose the agent type (AUTO or ON-CALL)
  • Tap Create to deploy your agent

No coding required! Your agent will be ready to use immediately.

What's the difference between AUTO and ON-CALL agents?

AUTO Agents: These agents automatically respond to every message in conversations where they're activated. Perfect for customer support, moderation, or always-on assistance.

ON-CALL Agents: These agents only respond when you specifically summon them by selecting them before sending a message. Great for specialized tasks or when you want control over when AI responds.

How do I add an agent to a conversation?

For AUTO agents:

  • Open the conversation
  • Tap the info/settings icon (top right)
  • Select "Manage Agents"
  • Toggle on the agent you want to activate

For ON-CALL agents: They're automatically available in all conversations. Just select the agent from the agent menu before sending a message that requires their expertise.

Can I browse agents created by others?

Yes! The Agent Marketplace features agents created by the Echo community:

  • Tap the Agents tab
  • Switch to Marketplace view
  • Browse by category or search for specific functionality
  • Tap any agent to see details and add it to your collection

How do I create a group conversation?

To create a group:

  • Go to the Conversations tab
  • Tap the + or New Group button
  • Enter a group name and description
  • Select members to add
  • Tap Create

You can create subgroups within groups for better organization!

How do I make a voice call?

Voice calling is easy:

  • Open a conversation
  • Tap the phone icon at the top
  • Wait for your contact to answer

Voice calls use high-quality audio streaming for crystal-clear conversations.

How do I delete or archive a conversation?

To archive: Swipe left on the conversation in your list and tap Archive.

To view archived conversations: Tap your profile ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Archived Conversations.

What is Jarvis?

Jarvis is your personal AI assistant built into Echo. Jarvis can help you:

  • Manage your messages and conversations
  • Check for unread messages
  • Send messages on your behalf
  • Get summaries of conversations
  • Navigate the app with natural language

Just chat with Jarvis like you would with a friend!

How do I use the calendar feature?

Echo's integrated calendar helps you stay organized:

  • Tap the Calendar icon
  • Tap + to create a new event
  • Add event details (title, date, description)
  • Save to track your upcoming activities

View your event statistics and monthly summaries in the Stats section.

How do I post to my profile?

Share your thoughts:

  • Go to your Profile tab
  • Tap New Post or the + button
  • Write your message (up to 2000 characters)
  • Tap Publish

Your posts appear on your profile and are visible to your connections.

Is Echo free to use?

Yes! Echo is completely free to download and use. You can:

  • Send unlimited messages
  • Make voice calls
  • Create unlimited AI agents
  • Join unlimited groups
  • Access the full agent marketplace

There are no subscriptions or hidden fees.

How is my data protected?

We take your privacy seriously:

  • Secure JWT authentication for all connections
  • Your messages are transmitted securely
  • We never sell your data to advertisers
  • You control who can see your profile and posts
  • You can block unwanted users at any time

Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

We appreciate bug reports! Please email us at bugs@echo.social with:

  • A description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce the bug
  • Your device model and iOS version
  • Screenshots if applicable

We'll investigate and fix it as quickly as possible.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account:

  • Go to Profile ‚Üí Settings
  • Scroll to Account section
  • Tap Delete Account
  • Confirm deletion

Note: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, messages, and agents will be deleted.
